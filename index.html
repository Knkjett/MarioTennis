How to play: BASICS

Movement

Standard Up/Down/Left/Right joystick movement controls. Up will move your character foward. Down will move your character backwards. Left will move your character left. Right will move your character left. The joystick includes diagonal movements for percision accuracy.

Top Spin Shot

The standard shot type in Mario Tennis Aces is the Topspin Shot, which is one of the quicker shots available to you in the game.

Slice Shot

In Mario Tennis Aces, the Slice Shot is slower and capable of bouncing closer to the net on the other end of the court.

Flat Shot

The Flat Shot is (as its name suggests) a flatter shot in Mario Tennis Aces capable of landing near the back of your opponent's side of the court.

Lob Shot

In Mario Tennis Aces, the Lob Shot arcs high in the air and lands near the back of your opponent's court.

Drop Shot

The Drop Shot is similar to the Slice Shot in Mario Tennis Aces in that it's slower and lands near the net, forcing your opponent to dive for the ball.

Trick Shot

To have alittle fun, try the Trick Shot in Mario Tennis Aces, this causes your character to move across the court and reach a ball that previously seems beyond reach.
